Welcome to Travel Website



Project Purpose: To provide tourism assistance and information, accommodation support, catering support, guide support, transportation, visa processing and travel assistance for international tourists and emergency support for the tourists.



Project Description: We all know Our Beloved Rangamati as the beauty queen of Bangladesh. Rangamati is blessed with natural resources and place of great tourism interest. We also have number of ethnic groups including Chakma, Marma, Tripura etc. They all have their own culture, history and resourceful language. The biggest artificial lake of South Asia “Kaptai Lake” is also at Rangamati. The heart of the city is located in the green hills surrounded by the lake. All these makes Rangamati a place of great tourism interest and tourist always keep visiting the beauty queen.



Our initiative is to provide best service to the tourists willing to visit Rangamati. We will be providing all the required services to the mentioned above in the purpose section.



We will also be signing MoU with administration, security service provider, accommodation service etc. We hope our project will be able to add value to the tourism service of Rangamati in a more effective and efficient way. We will be providing essential services just in a click!



So sit back and let us take all the hassle and pack your bag to have the greatest tourism experience.
